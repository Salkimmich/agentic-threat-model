# Limitations

This repository intentionally does not attempt to be a complete security standard.

## Scope limitations

- It is documentation-first and does not provide executable security tooling.
- It focuses on deployed agentic systems, not foundation model training internals.
- It provides generalized guidance and cannot replace environment-specific threat analysis.

## Method limitations

- ARS is a prioritization aid, not a quantitative risk model.
- Threat examples are representative patterns; they may not cover all edge cases.
- Framework mappings are practical crosswalks, not formal certification mappings.

## Operational limitations

- Organizations still need local policy decisions, legal review, and governance controls.
- Recommended controls may not be feasible in every architecture or budget profile.
- Detection recommendations assume availability of logging and monitoring infrastructure.

## How to use responsibly

- Treat this repository as a baseline and adapt to your environment.
- Document assumptions and residual risk decisions in your own process.
- Reassess threat models whenever architecture or permissions change materially.
